Marine Spatial Planning Guidelines

Comprehensive guidelines for implementing Local Marine Spatial Planning in Tanzania and the WIO region.

Core Principles

  1. Community-Centered - Local communities are central to planning and decision-making
  2. Evidence-Based - Decisions grounded in data and scientific research
  3. Inclusive - All stakeholder groups have a voice
  4. Adaptive - Plans can evolve based on new information and changing conditions
  5. Transparent - Open communication and accessible information

MSP Process Steps

  1. Scoping & Stakeholder Engagement - Define planning boundaries and identify key players
  2. Baseline Analysis - Map existing uses, resources, and priorities
  3. Visioning & Goal Setting - Articulate community aspirations
  4. Spatial Planning - Allocate zones for different uses
  5. Implementation - Activate management measures
  6. Monitoring & Review - Track progress and adapt as needed
