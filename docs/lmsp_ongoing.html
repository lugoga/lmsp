  • 350+ seaweed farmers organized in cooperatives

Dar Harbor & Offshore Resources Management

Central Dar es Salaam
2021 - Present

  • Landing site infrastructure improvements (4 sites)
  • Conflict reduction: 85% decrease in fishing disputes

Amboni-Mkuranga Coral Reef & Tourism MSP

Tanga Municipality, Tanga
2020 - Present
Marine protected area development balancing coral reef conservation with sustainable tourism. First major reef-focused MSP in Tanzania integrating dive operators and environmental groups.

  • 1,850 ha marine reserve with 3 management zones
  • 8 tourism operators in revenue-sharing agreements
  • Reef monitoring program: monthly fish surveys
  • Tourism revenue: $180,000/year to community fund
Implementation Progress
Planning: 100% | Implementation: 58% | Revenue Generation: 80%

Zanzibar Integrated Coastal Zone Management

Unguja & Pemba Islands, Zanzibar
2018 - Ongoing (Expansion phase)
Government-led MSP with focus on seaweed farming, artisanal fishing, and marine parks. Expanding from initial 2 districts to archipelago-wide coordination.
Current Status
  • 5 marine parks established (18,500 ha protected)
  • 12,000 seaweed farmers participating in MSP zones
  • 3 fish landing sites upgraded with modern facilities
  • Women groups: 800+ members in income-generating activities

Mtwara Coastal Fisheries & Oil/Gas Coordination

Mtwara Municipality & Mtwara Rural Districts
2021 - Present
Complex MSP integrating artisanal and industrial fisheries with emerging offshore oil and gas exploration. First MSP addressing energy sector integration in Tanzania.
Key Challenges & Solutions
  • Oil platforms: 2 designated development areas away from fishing zones
  • Fishing communities: 4,500+ engaged in alternative livelihood training
  • Environmental standards: 3 oil companies signed conservation protocols
  • Benefit-sharing: Local content policy in extraction agreements
Implementation Progress
Consultation: 85% | Zoning: 48% | Enforcement: 35%
Oil & Gas Energy Coordination Livelihood Transition

Lindi River Delta & Nearshore MSP

Lindi District, Lindi Region
2022 - Present
Ecosystem-based planning integrating river discharge patterns, mangrove protection, and sustainable fishing. Emerging initiative with strong government support and community participation.
Project Features
  • Seasonal fishing closure during river flood period (Nov-Mar)
  • 6,200 ha mangrove protection zone established
  • Community monitoring: 45 trained observers
  • Youth employment: 80+ in mangrove restoration
Implementation Progress
Early Phase: Zoning 60% | Implementation 42% | Monitoring 30%
